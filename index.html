About SmartLink

A simple digital solution for small-scale farmers

SmartLink is an agriculture-focused digital platform designed to reduce post-harvest food loss. We connect farmers directly with buyers such as retailers, restaurants, and food processors, helping farmers sell faster and earn more while reducing food waste.

How It Works

1. Farmer Uploads Produce

Farmers upload available crops and quantities using a mobile phone.

2. Buyer Places Order

Buyers browse listings and place orders directly.

3. Produce Delivered

Produce is delivered quickly, reducing waste and delays.
